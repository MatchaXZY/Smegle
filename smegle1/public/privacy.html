Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

Smegle is built with privacy first. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not sell your data to anyone.

1. What We Collect

We collect the minimum amount of data necessary to operate the Service:

IP addressAbuse prevention & legal compliance30 days

We do not store video streams, audio, or chat messages. All communication is peer-to-peer via WebRTC and passes directly between users' browsers.

2. Video & Audio

Video and audio streams are transmitted directly between users using WebRTC (peer-to-peer) technology. These streams do not pass through our servers and are never recorded or stored by Smegle.

However, other users may choose to record their screen independently. Be aware of this before showing anything you wouldn't want recorded.

3. Chat Messages

Chat messages are relayed through our signaling server in real time to enable communication. They are not stored, logged, or retained in any database. Once a session ends, messages are gone permanently.

4. Cookies

Smegle does not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. We may use a minimal session cookie to maintain your WebSocket connection. No third-party advertising networks have access to your data.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google STUN servers — used to establish peer-to-peer connections. Google may log connection requests per their own privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts — used to load the Inter typeface. Google may log font requests per their privacy policy.

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other advertising or tracking services.

6. Children's Privacy

Smegle is strictly for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect any data from minors. If you believe a minor has used the Service, please report it immediately using the in-app report button.

7. Law Enforcement

We will cooperate with law enforcement agencies when required to do so by law. This may include disclosing IP address logs or report data in response to valid legal requests.

8. Your Rights

Because we do not store personally identifiable information tied to an account, most data subject rights (access, deletion, portability) are satisfied automatically — there is no profile to delete. If you have a specific concern, use the in-app report function.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Service after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
